# 🎬 Netflix Content Analysis – Product Analysis for the Entertainment Industry  

## 1️⃣ Title  
**Netflix Content Analysis – Product Analysis for the Entertainment Industry**  

## 2️⃣ Executive Summary  

### 📊 Business Problem  
Content managers at Netflix need insights into which TV Shows and Movies are performing best, which ones are trending, and how frequently new content is added over time.

### ✅ Solution  
- Used **SQL** to query and analyse Netflix content data.  
- Developed a **Power BI Dashboard** for interactive visualisation of top content, trends, and engagement metrics.

### 🚀 Impact  
- Identified top-performing shows like “Stranger Things” and “Narcos”.  
- Revealed high content addition periods for strategic decisions.  


## 3️⃣ Business Problem  
How to track Netflix content performance by category, weekly rank, and viewership to help content strategy teams.


## 4️⃣ Methodology  
- Created table schema and inserted sample data.  
- Wrote multiple analytical SQL queries to answer key business questions.  
- Built visualisations in Power BI for better decision-making.

## 5️⃣ Skills Demonstrated  
- SQL: SELECT, WHERE, GROUP BY, HAVING, DISTINCT, UNION  
- Data Modelling: Table creation, primary keys  
- Power BI: Slicers, Drillthrough, Interactive dashboard  
- Business Reporting & Insights  

## 6️⃣ Results & Recommendations  
- Top 3 most viewed shows identified.  
- Recommended focusing on TV genres with high engagement during strategic periods.

## 7️⃣ Next Steps  
- Add customer ratings data for deeper insights.  
- Implement predictive analytics for future content trends.
