# 🚴 Bike Sales Dashboard – Power BI Project  

## 1️⃣ Title  
**Interactive Bike Sales Dashboard – Business Insights & Data Visualisation**

---

## 2️⃣ Executive Summary  

### 📊 Business Problem  
A bike retail business needs to analyse sales performance by region, product category, and time to optimise inventory management, marketing strategies, and sales growth.

### ✅ Solution  
- Created a Power BI dashboard that visualises bike sales performance across regions, time periods, and product categories.  
- Included interactive elements such as slicers for date range, region, and product category to enable flexible data exploration.

### 🚀 Impact  
- Enabled stakeholders to easily identify top-selling products and regions.  
- Provided actionable insights to support marketing focus and inventory restocking strategies.

---

## 3️⃣ Business Problem  
How to analyse and visualise bike sales data effectively to understand trends by region, category, and time, and assist in data-driven decision-making.

---

## 4️⃣ Methodology  
1. Imported the Bike Sales dataset into Power BI.  
2. Built interactive visualisations, including:
   - Sales trends over time (line chart)  
   - Sales by Region (map and bar chart)  
   - Category-wise sales breakdown  
   - Top performing products  
3. Implemented slicers for dynamic filtering by:
   - Region  
   - Product Category  
   - Time Period  

---

## 5️⃣ Skills Demonstrated  
- Data Modelling: Relationships, calculated columns, and measures  
- Data Visualisation: Interactive charts, tables, and slicers  
- Power BI Report Development  
- Business Analytics and Data Storytelling

---

## 6️⃣ Results & Business Recommendations  
- Discovered key regions driving the highest sales.  
- Identified top-selling product categories for targeted marketing.  
- Suggested a real-time dashboard for regular monitoring and faster decision-making.
